OUR HISTORY


HEX Construction Company (Nigeria) Limited, founded in April 1982, is
a leading and rapidly growing construction and civil engineering firm in
Nigeria. Originally focusing on renovations and subcontracting, the
company has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality, efficient
construction solutions. Over the past four decades, EL-ALAN has expanded
its services to include a broad range of construction needs across
residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

As part of a larger group of companies, EL-ALAN leverages world-class
ideas, skills, and innovative technology to provide integrated and value-
added services to clients across various sectors and budgets.ISTORY
